Customers

The Customers resource provides comprehensive management of customer data within the Upgrade.chat CRM system. This collection of endpoints allows for the creation, retrieval, updating, and deletion of customer records, enabling efficient customer relationship management.

Key features include:

  • Adding new customers with detailed information
  • Updating existing customer details
  • Retrieving customer information with filtering options
  • Removing customers from the system

ADD/UPDATE CUSTOMER

Create or update leads, contacts, and customers

POST {environment domain}/api/services/CRM/Contact/CreateOrUpdateContact
